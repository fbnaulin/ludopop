title: "Mapuche Weapons"
date: 2025-03-25
---
Mapuche characters have general training in every kind of weapon, crafted or improvised. And they specialize in a single one among these: short spear, long spear, bow and arrow, bola, or mace.

When using a specialized weapon in combat, the character gains a +2/per level instead of +1/per level bonus to attack and damage rolls with that weapon. Also, they have access to a special combat option, as seen in the table below.

Improvised and crafted
Improvised weapons break and do no damage, when rolling a 1 on damage roll. Weapons tagged as improvised could never be crafted.

Crafted weapons break when rolling the highest number in their damage roll, if its user chooses to do it, in which case they add a second damage roll to the total. Weapons tagged as crafted could never be improvised.
