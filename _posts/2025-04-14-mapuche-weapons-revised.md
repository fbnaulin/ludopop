Mapuche warriors use a varied arsenal of weapons.

**Attack**
When attacking with a weapon, roll a d20 to hit, and Damage dice, at the same time.
Add the corresponding AB to d20 roll. Attack hits and do that Damage if the result is equal or higher than target AC.
If the attack fails, but d20 is equal or lower than Damage dice, you cause a Detriment instead.

**Detriment**
Damage type. Value (1–3) is how severe could be a produced harmful situation. Player choice, cannot exceed value.

| **Severity** | **Mild**  | **Moderate** | **Severe**   |
|--------------|-----------|--------------|--------------|
| Slashing     | Bleeding  | Wounding     | Dismembering |
| Crushing     | Knockback | Stun         | Broke        |
| Piercing     | Prick     | Counter      | Impale       |
| Grappling    | Disarm    | Prone        | Constrict    |

**Making**
Improvised weapons break and do no damage, when rolling a 1 on damage roll. Weapons tagged as improvised could never be crafted. Crafted weapons break when rolling the highest number in their damage roll, if its user chooses to do it, in which case they add a second damage roll to the total. Weapons tagged as crafted could never be improvised.
